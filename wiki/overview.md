# Vault Overview

This vault is a personal second brain for turning captured material into reusable knowledge.

The current ingestion priorities are:

- web clips from Obsidian Web Clipper
- tweets and threads
- exported AI chats

Codex should preserve sources in `.raw/`, synthesize knowledge in `wiki/`, and keep the index, log, and hot cache current.
