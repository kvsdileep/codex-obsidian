# AI Finance Workflows

## Definition

AI finance workflows are repeatable analyst tasks packaged into AI-assisted processes, often connected to licensed market, company, or financial datasets.

## Why It Matters

Finance is a strong vertical for agent workflows because analysts repeat structured research, modeling, comparison, and reporting tasks every week.

## Example

- [[wiki/entities/Perplexity|Perplexity]] announced Perplexity Computer for Professional Finance with licensed data integrations and 35 dedicated finance workflows.
