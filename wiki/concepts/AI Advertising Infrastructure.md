# AI Advertising Infrastructure

## Definition

AI advertising infrastructure is the set of onboarding, verification, documentation, placement, and measurement systems needed for ads inside AI products.

## Why It Matters

If AI assistants become search, discovery, or recommendation surfaces, ad systems may become a major monetization layer. Advertiser verification and documentation are early signs of that shift.

## Example

- A tweet from Glenn Gabe says OpenAI launched a landing page for ads with signup, documentation, and a video guide.
