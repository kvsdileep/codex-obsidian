# Enterprise AI Governance

## Definition

Enterprise AI governance is the control layer for safe AI adoption, including hallucination control, security, IP and privacy boundaries, evaluation, approval workflows, and auditability.

## Why It Matters

For enterprise SDLC use cases, governance is not an add-on. It determines whether AI workflows can be trusted, scaled, and measured.
