# Agentic AI in the SDLC

## Definition

Agentic AI in the SDLC means using AI agents across software delivery activities such as requirements analysis, design, coding, testing, documentation, observability, and governance.

## Why It Matters

The enterprise value comes from repeatable workflow changes, not generic tool awareness. Training should therefore focus on practical use cases, validation, and role-specific workflows.
