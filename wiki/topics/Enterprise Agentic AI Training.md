# Enterprise Agentic AI Training

## Overview

Enterprise Agentic AI training teaches cross-functional teams to use agents, retrieval, observability, governance, and workflow automation in real business processes.

## Current Case

The Cheersin Interactive curriculum should be practical, SDLC-aligned, and modular. The raw chat suggests the first draft was too large at 2-hour sessions, and the next version should use 1-hour blocks.
